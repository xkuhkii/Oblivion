Common Fixes

Q: Why does it say virus detected? A: The reason behind this is called a false positive which means it automatically detected Oblivion as a virus, but it is harmless to your PC and has no malware/viruses

Q: Why does it keep kicking me from ROBLOX for unexpected client? A: No it is not Oblivion's fault. During Roblox's latest update they messed something up which causes random people to be kicked for no reason.

Q: Why does only a logo pop up and not the executor? A: You need to turn off your antivirus and then reopen Oblivion.

Q: Why does it say KRNL.dll is missing? A: Turn off your antivirus and then open this link, then download KRNL.dll, then add KRNL.dll to the Oblivion folder.
Q: How to fix Error Code 268.
1) Wait it out. The problem should resovle itself after many hours.
2) Uninstall and re-install Roblox. This is not guaranteed to work.
3) Open the run box, and type in "%LocalAppData%" and search for the file labelled "Roblox". After that. open it and delete the XML Documents and the cfg file. After that, attempt to restart Roblox and see if it works. Just change your Roblox setting back to how they were.
Q: Download link not working? A: Open the download link in an Incognito tab.
